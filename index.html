Lucy's Creative Tips

1. The Morning Page

Always spend 10 minutes sketching or writing lyrics before checking your phone to keep your inner channel clear.

2. Limit the Palette

True creativity flourishes under constraints. Try making a masterpiece using only three colours and one instrument.

3. Embrace Imperfection

At 43, I've learned that the most beautiful part of a song is often the accidental note. Let your art breathe.
